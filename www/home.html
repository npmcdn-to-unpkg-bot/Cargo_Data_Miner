Cargo Data Miner α

Cargo Data Miner is a R Shiny web application. It is developed by CSMX (Freighter Marketing). The initiative is to build a platform where everybody could query and analyze cargo data. Cargo Data Miner provides automatic data analysis and visualization for the following three databases:

Cargo IS

Cargo IS provides weight, charges and revenu information.

Read more.
Seabury

World air and ocean trade volumes are captured for over 200 countries from 1994 onwards. Data is available on a country to country level, with further breakdowns into customs districts for the largest freight consumption and production markets. Types of goods are provided at an unmatched level of detail for 2000 commodities.

Read more.
FlightRadar 24

Flightradar24 is a live flight tracker that shows air traffic in real time. Data from 2014 to 2015 are available in Airbus server.
